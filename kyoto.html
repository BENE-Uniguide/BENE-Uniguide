About Kyoto University

The University of Kyoto is one of Japan's top universities, known for its research and academic excellence.

International Undergraduate Program (Kyoto iUP)

The Kyoto iUP does not require prior knowledge of Japanese and offers courses entirely in English.

Academic Programs

Offers a wide range of programs in various fields including Arts, Sciences, and Engineering.

Rankings

Ranked #2 in Japan and #50 globally by QS World University Rankings.

Admission Requirements

  • Submit official high school transcripts
  • Meet English language proficiency requirements: TOEFL iBT 90, TOEFL PBT 580, IELTS 6.5
  • Optional: Additional test scores for ACT/SAT
  • Submit two letters of recommendation

Application Deadlines

December 4, 2023 (5 p.m. JST) for undergraduate admission.

Tuition Fees

JPY 535,800 per year for international students.

Additional Information

  • The University of Kyoto deadline for international students is December 4, 2023, at 5 p.m. JST for undergraduate admission.
