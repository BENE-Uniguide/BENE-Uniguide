London School of Economics and Political Science (LSE)

Location: London, England

About LSE

Established in 1895, the London School of Economics and Political Science is one of the leading social science universities in the world.

Academic Programs

Specializes in Social Sciences, Economics, Politics, Law, and Sociology.

Rankings

Ranked 4th in the UK according to Times Higher Education World University Rankings 2024.

Research Strengths

Strong in social sciences and economics research.

Admission Requirements

High academic standards with competitive entry requirements.

Application Deadlines

Vary by program, generally January for undergraduate applications.

Tuition Fees

£21,570 - £25,920 per year for international students.

Scholarships

Includes John C Phelan General Course scholarships, The BUTEX Scholarship.

Campus Facilities

Modern lecture halls, research centers, and libraries.

Student Life

Vibrant with numerous clubs, societies, and student activities.

Accommodation

Guaranteed housing for first-year students.

Support Services

Academic support, career services, and personal counseling.

Visa Information

Comprehensive visa support.

Cultural Integration

Rich multicultural environment with diverse student body.
