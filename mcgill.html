McGill University

Location: Montreal, Quebec, Canada

About McGill University

Established in 1821, McGill University is renowned for its research and diverse academic programs.

Academic Programs

Offers programs in Medicine, Law, Engineering, Arts, and Management.

Rankings

Ranked 31st globally according to QS World University Rankings.

Research Strengths

Strong in Biomedical research, Neuroscience, and Sustainability.

Admission Requirements

Requires a high school diploma, SAT range: 1290-1500, IELTS minimum score: 6.5.

Application Deadlines

January 15 for most programs.

Tuition Fees

CAD 18,000 - 48,000 per year for international students.

Scholarships

Includes McGill Entrance Scholarships, Major Entrance Scholarships.

Campus Facilities

Advanced research facilities, libraries, sports complex.

Student Life

Over 300 clubs and societies, student government, cultural festivals.

Accommodation

On-campus residences, off-campus housing support.

Support Services

International student services, academic advising, mental health resources.

Visa Information

Comprehensive visa application assistance.

Cultural Integration

Multicultural student associations, international orientation.
