University of Melbourne

Location: Melbourne, Victoria, Australia

About Melbourne

Established in 1853, the University of Melbourne is one of Australia's oldest and most prestigious universities.

Academic Programs

Offers a wide range of programs including Business, Law, Engineering, Medicine, and Arts.

Rankings

Ranked 33rd globally according to QS World University Rankings.

Research Strengths

Strong in fields such as Biomedical sciences, Environmental studies, and Engineering.

Admission Requirements

Requires a high school diploma, SAT range: 1300-1480, IELTS minimum score: 6.5.

Application Deadlines

November 30 for Semester 1 intake.

Tuition Fees

AUD 40,000 - 45,000 per year for international students.

Scholarships

Includes Melbourne International Undergraduate Scholarship, Graduate Research Scholarships.

Campus Facilities

State Library, research labs, sports complex.

Student Life

Over 200 clubs and societies, student union events.

Accommodation

On-campus residences available, with off-campus housing assistance.

Support Services

International student support office, orientation programs, language workshops.

Visa Information

Guidance provided on student visa application process.

Cultural Integration

Buddy programs, cultural events, and workshops.
