Université de Montréal

Location: Montreal, Quebec, Canada

About Université de Montréal

Established in 1878, Université de Montréal is known for its excellence in health sciences, social sciences, and environmental studies.

Academic Programs

Offers programs in Medicine, Law, Business, Arts and Sciences, and Engineering.

Rankings

Ranked 111th globally according to QS World University Rankings.

Research Strengths

Strong in Health sciences, Social sciences, and Environmental studies.

Admission Requirements

Requires a high school diploma, SAT range: 1200-1400, IELTS minimum score: 6.5.

Application Deadlines

February 1 for most programs.

Tuition Fees

CAD 17,000 - 25,000 per year for international students.

Scholarships

Includes UdeM Exemption Scholarship, Admission Scholarships.

Campus Facilities

Research centers, libraries, sports facilities.

Student Life

Active student associations, cultural events, sports clubs.

Accommodation

On-campus residences, off-campus housing assistance.

Support Services

International student office, academic support, health services.

Visa Information

Assistance with student visa applications.

Cultural Integration

French language support, multicultural events.
