University of Oxford

Location: Oxford, England

About Oxford

Established in 1096, the University of Oxford is one of the oldest and most prestigious universities in the world.

Academic Programs

Offers a wide range of programs including Humanities, Sciences, Social Sciences, and Medicine.

Rankings

Ranked 1st in the UK and globally according to Times Higher Education World University Rankings 2024.

Research Strengths

Known for pioneering research in sciences, medicine, and humanities.

Admission Requirements

High academic standards with A-level grades typically AAA; strong performance in entrance exams and interviews.

Application Deadlines

October 15 for undergraduate courses.

Tuition Fees

£28,950 - £44,240 per year for international students.

Scholarships

Includes Simon and June Li Undergraduate Scholarship, Reach Oxford Scholarship.

Campus Facilities

Extensive libraries, museums, and research labs.

Student Life

Numerous clubs, societies, and cultural activities.

Accommodation

Various college-based accommodations available.

Support Services

Comprehensive support including academic, career, and personal counseling.

Visa Information

Full support for visa applications.

Cultural Integration

Rich historical and cultural experience with international festivals and events.
