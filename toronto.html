University of Toronto

Location: Toronto, Ontario, Canada

About University of Toronto

Established in 1827, the University of Toronto is renowned for its excellence in research and diverse academic programs.

Academic Programs

Offers programs in Medicine, Business, Engineering, Law, and Arts & Science.

Rankings

Ranked 25th globally according to QS World University Rankings.

Research Strengths

Strong in fields such as Medicine, AI, and Environmental Studies.

Admission Requirements

Requires a high school diploma, SAT range: 1330-1500, IELTS minimum score: 6.5.

Application Deadlines

January 15 for most programs.

Tuition Fees

CAD 45,000 - 55,000 per year for international students.

Scholarships

Includes Lester B. Pearson International Scholarships, U of T Scholars Program.

Campus Facilities

Extensive libraries, research centers, athletic facilities.

Student Life

Over 1,000 student organizations, cultural events, leadership programs.

Accommodation

On-campus residences, off-campus housing options.

Support Services

International student center, academic advising, health services.

Visa Information

Comprehensive visa support and workshops.

Cultural Integration

Multicultural center, language exchange programs.
