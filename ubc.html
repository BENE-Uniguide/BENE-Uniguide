University of British Columbia

Location: Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada

About University of British Columbia

Established in 1908, the University of British Columbia is known for its excellence in research and diverse academic programs.

Academic Programs

Offers programs in Forestry, Business, Engineering, Arts, and Medicine.

Rankings

Ranked 34th globally according to QS World University Rankings.

Research Strengths

Strong in fields such as Environmental Science, Health Sciences, and Technology.

Admission Requirements

Requires a high school diploma, SAT range: 1250-1450, IELTS minimum score: 6.5.

Application Deadlines

January 15 for most programs.

Tuition Fees

CAD 40,000 - 50,000 per year for international students.

Scholarships

Includes International Major Entrance Scholarships, Outstanding International Student Award.

Campus Facilities

Cutting-edge research labs, student recreation center, libraries.

Student Life

Diverse student clubs, varsity sports, arts and cultural events.

Accommodation

On-campus housing, off-campus rentals.

Support Services

International student development, health and wellness services, career center.

Visa Information

Student visa support services.

Cultural Integration

Cultural diversity programs, international student mentoring.
