University of New South Wales

Location: Sydney, New South Wales, Australia

About UNSW

Established in 1949, the University of New South Wales is renowned for its strong emphasis on innovation and research.

Academic Programs

Offers programs in Engineering, Business, Law, Science, and Art & Design.

Rankings

Ranked 44th globally according to QS World University Rankings.

Research Strengths

Known for strengths in Engineering, Technology, and Social Policy.

Admission Requirements

Requires a high school diploma, SAT range: 1300-1460, IELTS minimum score: 6.5.

Application Deadlines

December 1 for Semester 1 intake.

Tuition Fees

AUD 39,000 - 48,000 per year for international students.

Scholarships

Includes UNSW International Scholarships, Future of Change Scholarship.

Campus Facilities

Advanced research labs, libraries, creative studios.

Student Life

Extensive student societies, arts and cultural activities.

Accommodation

University-managed residences, private accommodations.

Support Services

International student support, academic resources, health services.

Visa Information

Detailed visa guidance and support provided.

Cultural Integration

International student meet-ups, multicultural events.
